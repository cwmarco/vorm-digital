Hard Rules

Never mix fonts Brand logo always uses Fraunces. Product logo always uses Outfit Bold. No exceptions.
Optical lift is required The coral symbol has visual weight at the bottom. Always lift 4px when bare, 0px when in circle.
Tight gaps for product, breathing room for brand Product: 6px gap. Brand: 8-10px gap. Never tighten brand logo.
Don't use product logo in hero sections The circle + bold type competes with headlines. Use brand logo for marketing contexts.
Don't use brand logo in dense UI The bare coral + serif feels precious in utility contexts. Use product logo for apps.
Don't scale product logo gap linearly At XL sizes, increase gap to 8px max. Tighter isn't always better at scale.
